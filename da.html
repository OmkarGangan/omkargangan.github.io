Data Analysis

T-20 World Cup Data Analysis

In this project, I have divided Visualization as All time records, Season wise Records, Records in Knockout & Final, First & Last 3 season records and Indian Cricket Team records. First of all used selenium module for webscraping, pandas module for reading csv, grouping, label encoding, etc and plotly for visualization.
Tools & Technologies used: Python, Selenium, Pandas, Numpy, Plotly.
